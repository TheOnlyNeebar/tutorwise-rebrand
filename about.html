How TutorWise Works

We start with a free in-person assessment to identify your child’s unique learning needs. Then, we create a personalized tutoring plan and pair your child with an experienced TutorWise educator. Regular updates, skill tracking, and strong communication ensure visible progress and lasting results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Call or visit us to schedule a free evaluation. We’ll assess your child’s strengths and challenges and create a custom plan.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?

Yes — every session is one-on-one and customized to your child’s goals.

Can you help with multiple subjects?

Absolutely! We can support students across several subjects in their tutoring plan.

How often should sessions be scheduled?

We recommend at least two sessions weekly for consistent progress, but plans are flexible.
