Our Story

TutorWise began in 2006 as a home-based tutoring center known as Diviya Tutoring. Our roots were built on a deep love for education and a commitment to supporting the local community. Over the years, we’ve proudly supported hundreds of families in Brampton, helping students from all backgrounds reach their full academic potential.

As our community grew, so did we — evolving into TutorWise and moving to our dedicated location on Automatic Road. What started with living room whiteboards and dining room desks has become a trusted education hub, known for a warm, personal, and results-driven approach to learning.

Families who’ve been with us remember our holiday parties, fun summer camps, and lasting bonds. These aren’t just tutoring sessions — they’re stepping stones in a student’s journey. From IEP learners to high achievers, we treat every student as an individual.

At TutorWise, we believe in the power of a strong education. We know that academic results speak louder than words — and we’re proud to say our students consistently show growth, improved grades, and new confidence in themselves.
